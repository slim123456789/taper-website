Privacy Policy

Effective April 29, 2026

Taper is a swim training journal app for iOS. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. We collect only what we need to run the service. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads.

Information We Collect

When you use Taper we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and display name, provided by Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In.
  • Workout data — photos of whiteboards you capture, AI-parsed workout sets (distances, reps, rest intervals, stroke types), and total distances.
  • Journal entries — optional feel ratings, swim tags, and notes you attach to a workout.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Taper Pro subscription. Payment details are handled by Apple; we never see your card number.
  • Basic device info — iOS version and device model, used only for compatibility and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

  • Syncing your workouts across your devices via our cloud backend (Supabase).
  • Parsing whiteboard photos — images are sent to OpenAI's API to extract your workout. They are not stored by us after parsing and are not used to train OpenAI models.
  • Managing your subscription — we share your user ID with RevenueCat to verify your entitlement across devices.
  • Authentication — sign-in tokens from Apple or Google are exchanged with Supabase to create your session.

Third-Party Services

Taper uses the following services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — whiteboard photo parsing. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — authentication. Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your account and workout data is kept for as long as you have an active Taper account. When you sign out, your local data is cleared from the device. Cloud data remains and is restored when you sign back in.

To delete your account and all associated data, email support@taper.fit. We will complete the deletion within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Taper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided us data and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time:

  • Use the share feature in Taper to export your workouts.
  • Email support@taper.fit to request account deletion.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription via Settings in the app or through your Apple ID.

If you are in the EU or UK, you may have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page will reflect the latest revision. For material changes we will notify you via the app or by email.

Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email us at support@taper.fit.
