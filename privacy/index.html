01

Overview

Taper ("we", "our", "us") is a swim training journal app built for swimmers. This policy explains what data we collect when you use Taper on iOS, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

We collect only what we need to provide the service. We do not sell your personal data. We do not run ads.

02

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Taper:

Category
Details
Account
Your email address and display name, provided by Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In when you create an account.
Workout data
Photos of whiteboards you capture, AI-parsed workout sets (distances, reps, rest intervals, stroke types), workout names, and total distances.
Journal data
Optional entries you add: feel ratings, swim tags, and personal notes attached to a workout.
Subscription
Your subscription status (free or Pro) managed via RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details — these are handled by Apple.
Device info
iOS version and device model, collected by third-party services for crash reporting and compatibility purposes only.
03

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Taper:

Providing the service. We sync your workouts across your devices via our secure cloud backend (Supabase), so your training history is always up to date.

AI workout parsing. When you photograph a whiteboard, the image is sent to OpenAI's API to extract and structure your workout. Images are used only for this inference request and are not stored or used to train OpenAI models (per OpenAI's API data policy).

Subscription management. We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription entitlement across devices. Your Supabase user ID is shared with RevenueCat for this purpose only.

Authentication. Sign-in tokens from Apple or Google are exchanged with Supabase to create and maintain your session securely.

05

Data Retention

Your account and workout data is retained for as long as you have an active Taper account.

When you sign out of Taper, your workout data is immediately removed from your device. Your cloud data (workouts, journal) remains stored in Supabase and is restored when you sign back in.

To permanently delete all your data, email us at support@taper.fit and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

06

Children's Privacy

Taper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

07

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can:

Export your workouts using the share feature built into Taper.

Delete your account and data by emailing support@taper.fit.

Manage your subscription via the Customer Center in Taper's Settings screen or through your Apple ID subscription settings.

If you are located in the EU or UK, you may also have rights under GDPR or UK GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you via the app or by email.

Continued use of Taper after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
